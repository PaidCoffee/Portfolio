Welcome To My Portfolio

Hi, I'm a tech-educated creative with a background in Business Information Systems and a passion for all things visual, verbal, and user-focused. While my education is rooted in technology, my career interests lean strongly toward the creative side — especially marketing, UX, content creation, and communications. I'm not chasing titles or climbing ladders; I simply want to do meaningful work that keeps my brain active and puts bread on the table. With a flexible skill set shaped by both studies and hands-on experience, I’m open to a variety of roles, especially those that value creativity, clarity, and curiosity.
